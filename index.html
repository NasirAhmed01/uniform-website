    The Rise of

    A-1 UNIFORM

    For just 8 years, A-1 Uniforms has become a prominent name in the local market, serving some of the most prestigious institutions in Pakistan, including schools, colleges, and corporate companies. This partnership provides uniform solutions for hundreds of thousands of students and professionals, solidifying A-1 Uniforms' position as a trusted and leading uniform manufacturer.

    October 2016:

    A-1 Uniforms was sold by the Codrs to the current owner. Bringing in sales and inventory software allowed us to identify and carry more popular styles and colors, and sales increased greatly. Look how packed our first location was!

    June 2017:

    A new owner of the 906 2nd St. building declined to renew our lease in order to expand their business, so we needed to move. After remodeling a beautiful new space, A-1 Uniforms relocated to 304 2nd Street.
